Justin Joseph
Justin Joseph, a storyteller and podcaster from Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, has built a strong presence online with 71.5K YouTube subscribers and over 141K followers on Instagram. His passion for storytelling is evident in every plot he crafts, captivating audiences by blending imagination with reality. Each episode he produces is a new chapter, filled with voices and stories that leave a lasting impact. Having started his journey as an animator before becoming a YouTuber and founder of an internet company, Justin also created an Android app. Now, fully embracing his love for storytelling and podcasting, his words, "This is who I always was, it just took me time to meet myself" reflect the deep connection and pride he feels for his work. He pours his heart into his storytelling passion, serving as an inspiration to artists everywhere.
Phir kya hua?
“Yeh kahani nhi, Kahaniyon ka ek khel hai” PHIR KYA HUA? is a unique series created by Justin Joseph, where he narrates a story shaped by the audience's choices. Viewers control the direction of the plot by selecting one of the options presented by Justin. The audience determines the next steps in the story by liking (voting on) a comment representing their preferred option across platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook. Voting remains open for 48 hours following the release of each episode, and the option with the most votes will guide the next phase of the main storyline.
